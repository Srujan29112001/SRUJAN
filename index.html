About Me

Hi! I'm K Srujan, an AI Engineer and Robotics Specialist with a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology and consciousness. Currently pursuing my Master's at Thapar Institute, I blend cutting-edge technical skills with philosophical depth gained from my unique spiritual journey.

My technical journey began with a transformative 6-month internship at DRDO-DRDL, where I worked on indigenous defense projects implementing YOLOv7 on NVIDIA Jetson platforms for real-time aerial object detection. This experience, under Dr. Akula Naresh's mentorship, taught me the importance of precision and innovation in critical applications.

Following DRDO, I embarked on a 12-month spiritual and philosophical sabbatical that profoundly shaped my approach to problem-solving. This retreat wasn't an escape but an intense period of consciousness exploration, studying ancient wisdom alongside modern philosophy, developing adaptive decision-making frameworks that now enhance my technical work.

This unique combination of technical excellence and philosophical depth allows me to approach AI and robotics not just as technical challenges, but as opportunities to create meaningful impact. Whether developing computer vision systems, building robotic solutions, or exploring space and biotech applications, I bring a holistic perspective that considers both technological advancement and human consciousness.

My work spans three primary domains: Artificial Intelligence (with focus on deep learning and computer vision), Robotics (emphasizing control systems and real-world applications), and Space & Biotech Research (exploring the intersection of technology with life sciences). Each project reflects my commitment to innovation that serves humanity's broader evolution.
